Miscellaneous

Hello! I am currently a 2nd-year PhD student in the Computer Science department at Stanford University, where I am extremely fortunate to be advised by Professor Carlos Guestrin. I am also affiliated with the Stanford AI Lab and Stanford Machine Learning Group. My research interests currently revolve around enabling safer and more practical machine learning algorithms, with a particular focus on model evaluation, fairness, as well as applications to healthcare. I am grateful for my research to be supported by the NDSEG Fellowship starting Fall 2021.
